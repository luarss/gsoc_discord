Among the proposed ideas, relocation between cells seems the most practical to me and could potentially bring meaningful improvements. One advantage of this approach is that it does not introduce additional power overhead (at least not directly, although cell displacement could still potentially lead to worse power performance).

For the complement transformation, I am not sure how many nets in practice have both the driver and the loads as buffers, so I wonder whether this case occurs frequently enough to have a noticeable impact. If not, I think we could still insert two inverters to reverse the polarity, though this would introduce some additional power overhead.
